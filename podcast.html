The Consilient Mind Podcast

Long-form conversations for a world of short answers.

Clinicians, researchers, philosophers, artists, and practitioners meet around the questions that resist a single discipline.

A note on listening

The podcast is not a shortcut to certainty.

It is a place for sustained attention: to ambiguity, disagreement, lived experience, emerging science, and the questions that become more interesting the longer we stay with them.

An interdisciplinary home for science, story, reflection, and the shared work of becoming more fully human.
